# Jobs-To-Be-Do Statements

### Master JTBD Statement
>***When*** the noise of the day gets too loud and I need a moment to step back,<br />***I want to*** be met with a lyric that speaks to where I am right now,<br />***so I can*** find a moment of clarity, reflect, and carry on.

---

### Functional Jobs

### JTBD-1: Daily Lyric Discovery
>***When*** I begin my day,<br />***I want to*** be greeted with a lyric I hadn't thought about for a while,<br />***so I can*** start from a place of intention rather than noise.

### JTBD-2: In-the-Moment Reflection
>***When*** the day is overwhelming and I need a quiet moment,<br />***I want to*** arrive at a lyric that speaks to how I feel without having to search for one,<br />***so I can*** step back and ground myself quickly.

### JTBD-3: Discovering What Resonates
>***When*** I land on a lyric that strikes a chord,<br />***I want to*** mark it as meaningful and return to it easily,<br />***so I can*** build a personal shortlist of words that do the most work for me.

### JTBD-4: Growing the Collection
>***When*** I discover a song with words worth keeping,<br />***I want to*** add it to my collection without friction,<br />***so I can*** ensure the library grows with me rather than staying frozen in time.

### JTBD-5: Finding the Right Words
>***When*** I am struggling to name how I feel,<br />***I want to*** browse or filter by mood or theme,<br />***so I can*** find the lyric that best reflects where I am in that moment.

---

### Emotional Jobs

### EJ-1: Feeling Grounded
>***When*** the world is moving too fast,<br />***I want to*** land on something honest and familiar,<br />***so I can*** feel less adrift and more like myself.

### EJ-2: Feeling Less Alone
>***When*** I am going through something difficult,<br />***I want to*** read words that someone else wrote about the same feeling,<br />***so I can*** feel held by the music rather than isolated by the moment.

### EJ-3: Starting Well
>***When*** I sit down at the start of a new day,<br />***I want to*** be offered something worth sitting with,<br />***so I can*** carry a thought into the day that is mine, not the algorithm's.

---

### Social Jobs

### SJ-1: Sharing What Resonates
>***When*** a lyric says something I cannot,<br />***I want to*** share it simply and without ceremony,<br />***so I can*** offer something real to the people around me who might need it.

### SJ-2: Leaving Something Behind
>***When*** I think about the people who mattered to me,<br />***I want to*** curate a collection that reflects what I believed in,<br />***so I can*** leave something worth passing on.
