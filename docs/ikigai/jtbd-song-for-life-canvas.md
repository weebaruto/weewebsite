# Jobs-To-Be-Done Canvas

| **Component** | **Description** |
|--------------|-----------------|
| **Job Statement (Core JTBD)** | ***When*** the noise of the day gets too loud and I need a moment to step back,<br />***I want to*** be met with the lyrics to a song that speaks to where I am right now,<br />***so I can*** find a moment of clarity, reflect, and carry on. |
| **Functional Jobs** | • Be served a full song's lyrics on arrival — no searching, no setup<br />• Browse or filter by mood, theme, or artist when needed<br />• Mark songs whose lyrics resonate and return to them easily<br />• Add new songs to the collection without friction<br />• Read a full set of lyrics in a clean, distraction-free environment |
| **Emotional Jobs** | • Feel grounded when overwhelmed<br />• Feel less alone in difficult moments<br />• Begin the day from intention, not noise<br />• Be reminded of who you are when you have forgotten |
| **Social Jobs** | • Share the lyrics to a song with someone who needs it, simply and without ceremony<br />• Build a collection that reflects something worth leaving behind |
| **Pain Points / Struggling Moments** | • The song collection is static — a bull-clipped pile that doesn't travel well<br />• Finding the right song in a hard moment takes effort you don't have<br />• No way to stumble across something serendipitously<br />• The reflection ritual has no dedicated space — it gets crowded out<br />• Adding new songs is manual, so the collection stops growing |
| **Desired Outcomes** | • A song whose lyrics feel relevant, not random<br />• A moment of quiet that takes seconds to reach<br />• A living collection that grows and changes with you<br />• Something worth sharing when words of your own fall short |
| **Success Metrics** | • Returns daily without being prompted<br />• Time-to-reflection measured in seconds<br />• Collection grows over time<br />• Marked / saved songs accumulate — evidence of resonance |
| **Drivers** | • Music as a lifelong grounding mechanism<br />• The reflection ritual developed through 90-in-90 worth preserving<br />• Belief that the right song at the right moment carries real weight<br />• A dog-eared bull-clip book that deserves better |
| **Constraints** | • Must be frictionless — no account, no configuration, no barrier<br />• Must work in a moment of stress — fast, quiet, simple<br />• Curation cannot feel like work or it won't happen<br />• Must feel personal, not algorithmic |
| **Opportunity Areas** | • Random daily song (lyrics) served on page load — zero clicks<br />• Mood and theme tagging for intentional browsing<br />• Bookmarking with a single tap<br />• Clean, beautiful reading experience — typography first<br />• Simple sharing (copy, image card, or link)<br />• A growing personal archive, not a playlist |
