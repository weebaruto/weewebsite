# Jobs-To-Be-Done – [Reliability Ravi](../../personas/persona-reliability-ravi.md)

## Master JTBD Statement
***When*** ensuring reliability across distributed, production systems,\
***I want to*** maintain consistent, trustworthy, and actionable telemetry at scale,\
***so I can*** detect issues early, reduce operational burden, and support fast, accurate incident response.

---

## JTBD-1: Maintain High-Quality Telemetry Pipelines
***When*** operating OpenTelemetry collectors and ingestion pipelines,\
***I want to*** ensure they are stable, scalable, and consistent across environments,\
***so I can*** trust the data and avoid gaps during incidents.

---

## JTBD-2: Detect Problems Before They Escalate
***When*** monitoring system health,\
***I want to*** identify anomalies, regressions, or reliability risks early,\
***so I can*** prevent incidents and reduce customer impact.

---

## JTBD-3: Provide Standardized Reliability Practices
***When*** supporting engineering teams,\
***I want to*** give them clear instrumentation, SLO, and alerting standards,\
***so I can*** reduce fragmentation and raise the baseline for reliability.

---

## JTBD-4: Enable Fast, Correlated Incident Investigation
***When*** responding to high-severity incidents,\
***I want to*** access correlated logs, metrics, and traces in one place,\
***so I can*** diagnose root cause quickly and restore service health.

---

## JTBD-5: Reduce Operational Toil
***When*** managing observability infrastructure and support requests,\
***I want to*** reduce repetitive configuration and manual workflows through automation,\
***so I can*** focus on reliability improvements rather than firefighting.

---
