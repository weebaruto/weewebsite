# Reliability Ravi - JTBD Statements

### Master JTBD Statement
>***When*** ensuring reliability across distributed, production systems,<br />
***I want to*** maintain consistent, trustworthy, and actionable telemetry at scale,<br />
***so I can*** detect issues early, reduce operational burden, and support fast, accurate incident response.

### JTBD-1: Maintain High-Quality Telemetry Pipelines
>***When*** operating OpenTelemetry collectors and ingestion pipelines,<br />
***I want to*** ensure they are stable, scalable, and consistent across environments,<br />
***so I can*** trust the data and avoid gaps during incidents.

### JTBD-2: Detect Problems Before They Escalate
>***When*** monitoring system health,<br />
***I want to*** identify anomalies, regressions, or reliability risks early,<br />
***so I can*** prevent incidents and reduce customer impact.

>***When*** supporting engineering teams,<br />
***I want to*** give them clear instrumentation, SLO, and alerting standards,<br />
***so I can*** reduce fragmentation and raise the baseline for reliability.

>***When*** responding to high-severity incidents,<br />
***I want to*** access correlated logs, metrics, and traces in one place,<br />
***so I can*** diagnose root cause quickly and restore service health.

### JTBD-5: Reduce Operational Toil
>***When*** managing observability infrastructure and support requests,<br />
***I want to*** reduce repetitive configuration and manual workflows through automation,<br />
***so I can*** focus on reliability improvements rather than firefighting.
